Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TaskFlow Invest collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

What We Collect

  • Account information: name and email.
  • Authentication data: password is stored as a hashed value on the server.
  • Task data you create: titles, descriptions, due dates, status, and related fields.
  • Basic technical data: your browser and device information for security and troubleshooting.

How We Use Data

  • To provide the service (sign-in, task storage, and task management).
  • To secure accounts and prevent abuse.
  • To improve reliability and performance.

Cookies

We use an HttpOnly session cookie to keep you signed in. This cookie is used for security and cannot be accessed by JavaScript.

Data Storage

Your account and tasks are stored on the server in a database. If you delete your tasks, they are removed from your account. Backups may retain historical copies for a limited period.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only when required to operate the service (for example, hosting infrastructure) or when required by law.

Your Choices

  • You can update your profile information in the app.
  • You can delete tasks you created.

توضح هذه السياسة كيفية جمع واستخدام وحماية معلوماتك عند استخدام TaskFlow Invest.

المعلومات التي نجمعها

  • معلومات الحساب: الاسم والبريد الإلكتروني.

كيف نستخدم البيانات

ملفات تعريف الارتباط (Cookies)

نستخدم Cookie جلسة (HttpOnly) للمحافظة على تسجيل الدخول. هذا النوع غير قابل للوصول عبر JavaScript.

التخزين

المشاركة

لا نبيع بياناتك الشخصية. تتم مشاركة البيانات فقط عند الضرورة لتشغيل الخدمة أو عند وجود متطلبات قانونية.
